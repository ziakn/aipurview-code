# Niş AI Governance Startup'ları

## Amaç

5 niş AI governance startup'ını derinlemesine incele: Saidot, Trustible, Modulos, Monitaur, Asenion (eski Fairly AI). Küçük oyuncular en hızlı yenilik yapar.

## Ne yapıldı

### Saidot (saidot.ai)
- **Azure AI Foundry integration** — bidirectional governance handshake
- **Knowledge graph** — risks/controls auto-update on model changes
- **14 most common AI agent risks** taxonomy

### Trustible (trustible.ai)
- **"Agentic Governance" pillar** — context-aware orchestration
- **Risk-based triage** — fast-track lanes for low-risk
- **9-question agentic risk assessment**

### Modulos AI (modulos.ai, CHF 8.7M raise)
- **Scout AI Assistant** — conversational ISO 42001 copilot (named!)
- **Agent inventory primitives** — tools, memory, credentials, authority scope
- **OWASP Top 10 Agentic** as a first-class framework
- **Monetary risk quantification** instead of qualitative matrices

### Monitaur (monitaur.ai)
- **Automate Record** — ~40% auto-evidence rate
- **Five monitoring domains** for agents: functionality, operational consistency, human factors, security threats, compliance adherence
- **Guided Risk Assessment + Evidence Mapping** (Aug 2025)

### Asenion (Fairly AI rebranded, Jun 2025)
- **Testing Agents** — patent-pending autonomous fairness/privacy/security probes
- **Red-Teaming-in-a-Box**
- IBM watsonx.ai partnership

## Industry Trends (5 startup ortak)

- "Agentic Governance" artık named product pillar — feature değil, market segment
- OWASP Top 10 for Agentic Applications adoption başladı
- Knowledge graph = dominant data architecture
- Hyperscaler integrations table stakes (Azure Foundry, IBM watsonx)
- Auto evidence-to-control mapping = next automation frontier
- Agent inventory primitives (tools/memory/credentials/authority) emerging schema

## Top 5 VerifyWise için öneri

1. **Agent Inventory Primitives** (Modulos)
2. **Hyperscaler Governance Plugins** (Saidot — Foundry/Bedrock/Vertex)
3. **Auto Evidence-to-Control Mapping** (Monitaur — ~40% target)
4. **Testing Agents / Eval Probes** (Asenion)
5. **Risk-Based Triage + Multi-Role Orchestration** (Trustible)
